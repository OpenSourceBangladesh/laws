**Date of Publication:** [ 16th December, 1943 ]

## Preamble

WHEREAS it is expedient to apply the [Soldiers (Litigation) Act, 1925](/laws/volume-7/act-135 "Soldiers (Litigation) Act, 1925"), to the Chittagong Hill-tracts;

It is hereby enacted as follows:-

## Sections/Articles

### Short title

1. This Regulation may be called [THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS LAWS (No. III) REGULATION, 1943](/laws/volume-10/act-1335 "THE CHITTAGONG HILL-TRACTS LAWS (No. III) REGULATION, 1943").

### Application of Act IV of 1925 to the Chittagong Hill- tracts

2. The [Soldiers (Litigation) Act, 1925](/laws/volume-7/act-135 "Soldiers (Litigation) Act, 1925"), shall apply to the Chittagong Hill-tracts.
